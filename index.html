Frequently Asked Questions

How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
Is this website free to use?
Yes, many of our lessons and exercises are free to access. However, some advanced courses and premium features may require a subscription.
Do I need to create an account?
You can browse some content without an account, but creating an account allows you to track your progress, access full courses, and receive personalized recommendations.
How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can build your vocabulary by practicing daily lessons, using flashcards, completing quizzes, reading articles, and engaging in listening and speaking exercises regularly.
Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Yes, we provide certificates for certain completed courses. After successfully finishing the course requirements, you can download your certificate.
